About Our Temple

There are many Temples dedicated to Lord Ayyappa in Kerala, the holiest being the one at sacred Sabari Hills. The sphere of influence of Lord Ayyappa had spread beyond the boundaries of Kerala and new Ayyappan Temples had started springing up at various places not only in the other States of the South and North India but even in other countries also. Such Ayyappa Shrine to have come up outside Kerala State in the Temple at Kumbalgodu, Bangalore, the installation ceremony of which was conducted on 17th May 2014.

Within a short span of time, this temple has grown as a Sri kshethra and became a focal point of worship to 1000s of devotees not only in Kumbalgodu (Bangalore), but also all over the State & the world.
