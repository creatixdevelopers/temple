What We Do

Education Support

Education is the only powerful mantra, not only changes once life but the next generations to come. INDIA today stands at 74% literacy rate, still below the world average of 84%. Most of us are privileged to get the best-in-class education, but this opportunity is still far sighted to many kids.

Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® selects remotest villages, poor colonies to support 1000 Govt school /Differently abled/Orphan homes and provide them with school bags, slippers, stationary, Plates and glasses for the Mid-Day meals program, provide basic infrastructure- Fans, lights and drinking water etc, partner with other NGOs to provide college fees in special cases.

Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® are making a difference by continuing this journey for a better INDIA, where these kids will lead the country's future.

Health Support

Most Senior Citizens don't even go to hospital inspite of their old age aliments fearing doctors would ask more money. We identify and setup basic health camps for regular health/eye check-up and give them free medicines or link them with any hospital where Govt schemes are available.

Gaushala Support

Gau Seva or taking care of cows has been an integral part of the Vedic culture and heritage. To protect and care for cows is considered as one of the most revered services. At Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ®, we have a Gaushala and presently taking care of 25 cows.

We have a team of well-trained and skilled staff dedicated to look after the herd. We provide:

  • Quality fodder and regular supplements
  • Clean and hygienic shelter
  • Large field for cows to graze freely
  • Routine medical check-up

Go Green Initiatives

Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® aims to plant 1000 saplings every year, distribute clay Ganesha, Support awareness programs with Pachagavya trainings, Partner with like-minded NGOs to manufacture Cost Effective Jute bags for NGOs.

Homeless Support

In our country more than 15% people do have not shelter. They have no money to purchase their daily needs. For these people at the time of festivals we provide food, distribution of bed sheets. This support is given to 100’s of people by Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® every year.

Annadanam Support

Annam ParaBrahma Swaroopam - food is life, Annadanam is the highest form of seva in Santayana dharma. Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® members celebrate their Birthdays in a unique way to support dharmic old ages homes/ orphan homes/ Satrams by sponsoring this event. We offer food packets to street homeless, old age people during Navaratri, Karthika Masam etc.

Veda Pathashaala

Protecting and Restoring rural VedaPathasa, with infrastructure and Groceries

Saamuhika Pooja events

Sree Dharmashasta Ayyappa Religious Trust ® conducts "Saamuhika Pooja Events" during major festivals Sravana Masam, Dasara Navaratri, Kaarthika Maasam, Maha Sivaratri, Ugadi, Vinayaka Chavithi etc. Inspiring our women to practice our culture, educating on various challenges we face today, give us immense satisfaction.
